Privacy Policy – Glance Trans

Effective Date: August 19, 2025

1. Introduction

Glance Trans ("we", "our", or "the Extension") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle any data that may be collected when you use the Glance Trans extension.

2. Data Collection

Glance Trans does not collect or store any personal information from users by default. The extension operates primarily locally in your browser to provide translation functionality.

  • The extension may access the content of the current web page only when you use it (e.g., click the extension button to translate).
  • No page content is stored or sent to us.

3. Third-Party Services

Glance Trans may use third-party translation APIs (e.g., Google Translate) to perform translations.

  • These services may process text you submit for translation.
  • We do not control how third-party services handle data, so please refer to their privacy policies.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

5. Cookies

Glance Trans does not use cookies.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any minimal data transmitted to third-party APIs. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.
